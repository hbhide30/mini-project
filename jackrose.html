JACK AND ROSE

DESCRIPTION: Jack and Rose, the multi-colored lovebirds, are a charming and affectionate pair. Jack, the male, displays vibrant colors in shades of green, red, and blue, while Rose, the female, boasts soft pastel hues. Their expressive, beady eyes reflect their deep bond and playful nature. Lovebirds, known for their lifelong partnerships, share a unique and loving connection. With their joyful chirps and synchronized flights, they enchant all who witness their love story.
